The dominant masculine narratives too often become a substitute for feminine narratives There is no escape from the male gaze, except in the female gaze. It thus becomes essential to put out more female narratives. Narratives of the corrupt female, of the crude female, of the unscrupulous female, of the selfish female, of the powerful female. Women taking over all spheres of life, women occupying space. Including the world of the bad. Dante places the neutral angels in the quarrel between God and Satan in ‘Limbo’: a waiting for hell. The feminine condition is no different. We are walking out of Limbo and standing on the pedestal of Judgement. The judgement that comes from the privilege of power and of will.
